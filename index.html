Definitions & assumptions
2026 NOWTotal tickets sold to date for that specific ticket type only. Saturday row = Saturday single-day tickets only. CERA and Combi are shown in separate rows — not included here to avoid double-counting.
2025 @D-XXNumber of 2025 tickets sold at the equivalent point in the sales cycle — the same number of days before the festival as today. Allows a fair like-for-like comparison with 2026.
2025 FinalTotal 2025 tickets sold across the entire season, all waves combined. Used as the benchmark for how far along 2026 sales are.
Δ vs FinalHow far 2026 current sales are relative to the 2025 final total. A figure above 100% means 2026 has already surpassed 2025's full-season sales.
CapacityMaximum tickets available per day. Source: JM Pricing sheet, cells H68–H70. This figure already accounts for Combi ticket allocations within the total.
% Filled2026 NOW tickets ÷ Capacity × 100. Based on single-day tickets only (Combi not re-added here as it is already included in the capacity figure).
Current WaveThe active sales wave for each ticket type, determined by the subtitle of the most recently purchased ticket in the raw Paylogic data.
Sold (wave)Number of tickets sold specifically within the current active wave. Does not include previous waves.
QuotaThe maximum number of tickets allocated to the current wave. Sourced live from the JM26 Capacities tab in the pricing spreadsheet. Updates when you press ↻ Refresh.
Att. Est. 2026Estimated attendance per day = day tickets + CERA tickets for that day + Combi tickets (Combi holders attend all three days). This is a raw ticket count — no no-show deduction is applied.
Att. 2025 FinalActual attendance recorded at Jazz Middelheim 2025 per day. Source: historical records.

Combi (all days)A Combi ticket grants access to all three festival days. Combi holders are counted in the attendance estimate for every day they attend. Shown in its own row in the ticket column.

Net revenue breakdown — 2026 vs 2025 (gross → fees → VAT → net)
2026: 2% fee. 2025: 2% + €0.45/ticket. VAT 6% on gross both years.
Definitions & assumptions
Gross revenueThe total face value of tickets sold, before any deductions. This is the price the customer paid.
Fees (2%)Paylogic platform fee charged at 2% of gross revenue. Applied to both 2026 and 2025 figures for consistency.
VAT (6%)Belgian VAT on event tickets, calculated at 6% of gross revenue.
Net revenueWhat Jazz Middelheim receives after fees and VAT. Formula: Gross − 2% fee − (2025 only: €0.45/ticket) − 6% VAT.
2025 fee differenceIn 2025, Paylogic charged an additional fixed fee of €0.45 per ticket on top of the 2% platform fee. This extra charge does not apply in 2026.
Net 2025 @same periodNet revenue 2025 calculated using only tickets sold up to the same DTF point as today's 2026 data. Allows a fair year-on-year comparison.
Net 2025 FullNet revenue for the entire 2025 season — all tickets, all waves.
2026 vs 2025 FinalHow much of the 2025 full-season net revenue has already been reached in 2026. A figure above 100% means 2026 net revenue has already surpassed the entire 2025 season.
Cumul. net revenue — allCumulative net revenue chart showing day-by-day running total for all ticket types combined. A flat line means no tickets were sold on those days.
Cumul. net revenue — per typeSeparate cumulative net revenue for each ticket type (Saturday, Sunday, Monday, Combi). Excl. CERA, which is shown in the revenue table but not charted separately.
Daily revenue bar chartGross revenue per individual day. Each bar represents one calendar day. 2026 and 2025 are shown as separate stacked groups (not stacked on top of each other).

Definitions & assumptions
Current WaveThe active sales wave for each ticket type. Determined by looking at the most recently purchased ticket in the raw Paylogic data — specifically the wave name (subtitle) on the ticket with the lowest DTF value. This matches what is shown in the live ticket shop.
Sold (wave)Number of tickets sold specifically within the current active wave only. Does not include tickets from previous waves.
QuotaThe maximum number of tickets allocated to the current wave. Sourced live from the JM26 Capacities sheet. If you update a quota in that sheet and press ↻ Refresh, the dashboard updates automatically.
% QuotaSold in current wave ÷ Wave quota × 100. Indicates how full the current wave is.
Past waves soldTotal tickets sold in all previous waves (before the current active wave). Shown as the green segment in the capacity bar chart.
Total soldAll tickets sold for this type across all waves combined, up to today.
CapacityMaximum tickets available per ticket type across all waves. For single-day tickets, sourced from JM Pricing H68–H70. For individual wave types, sourced from the JM26 Capacities tab.
Sales vs capacity chartStacked bar showing the breakdown of total capacity: green = past waves (sold), orange = current wave (sold), grey = not yet sold (remaining capacity).
% labels on barsEach coloured segment shows its share of total capacity as a percentage. Segments smaller than 4% do not show a label to avoid clutter.
% of capacity soldThe bar chart on the right shows overall fill rate per ticket type: total tickets sold ÷ total capacity.

Definitions & assumptions
Ticket Sales tableShows 2026 sales, 2025 same-period comparison, and 2025 final totals per individual ticket type. Each row is a separate product — no aggregation is done between rows.
Capacity tableShows the total ticket capacity per type and how much has been sold. Capacity is sourced from the JM26 Capacities sheet and updates on ↻ Refresh.
Wave Progress tableBreaks down the current wave status: which wave is active, how many sold in that wave, the quota, and cumulative past-wave sales. Useful for understanding where each ticket type sits in its sales lifecycle.
Attendance tableEstimated attendance per festival day. Calculated as: day tickets + CERA tickets for that day + Combi tickets (since Combi holders attend all days). Raw count — no no-show deduction.
2025 FinalFull-season 2025 figures used as the reference benchmark. These are fixed historical values and do not change.
Δ 2026 vs 2025 FinalCompares the 2026 attendance estimate against the 2025 actual attendance. A bar at 100% means 2026 is on track to match 2025; above 100% means exceeding it.
Row orderTables are ordered as: Combi → Saturday → Sunday → Monday → Sat-CERA → Sun-CERA → Mon-CERA. This matches the order in the Status at a Glance table on the Overview tab.
